## The High-Value Play: SealBet

**What**: Non-custodial, peer-to-peer prediction markets on TON, native to Telegram.

**Why it's the $100M opportunity**:
1. Polymarket did $3.74B volume in one month
2. TON has 35M wallets and no serious prediction protocol
3. 950M Telegram users = distribution
4. MemeSeal proof integration = unique moat
5. Non-custodial = legally defensible

**Revenue model**:
- 0.5% fee on winning payouts
- 10% share of LP earnings
- Market creation fees

**At scale (Polymarket-level)**:
- $500M monthly volume × 0.5% = $2.5M/month revenue
- That's $30M/year from fees alone

## Why NOT MemeMart Now

MemeMart is a viable $500K/year business, but:

1. **Linear scaling** - More sales = more work
2. **Operational burden** - Inventory, shipping, returns, customs
3. **Lower ceiling** - $500K/year vs $100M+/year
4. **Distraction** - Every hour on MemeMart is an hour not on SealBet

**Revisit when**:
- SealBet generating $10K+/month
- Revenue to hire operations help
- Time/bandwidth available
